1997-1991 - Birth and Early life

Marshall Bruce Mathers III was born on October 17, 1972

Friends and family remember Eminem as a happy child, but "a bit of a loner" who was often bullied.

Eminem heard his first rap song on the Breakin' soundtrack, a gift from Debbie's half-brother Ronnie Polkinghorn.

After spending three years in ninth grade due to truancy and poor grades, he dropped out of Lincoln High School at age 17.

Eminem sneaked into neighboring Osborn High School with friend and fellow rapper Proof for lunchroom freestyle rap battles.

1992-1997 - Early career, family struggles

Eminem formed Soul Intent with rapper Proof and other childhood friends.

Eminem and Proof teamed up with four other rappers to form The Dirty Dozen (D12), who released their first album Devil's Night in 2001

Eminem was soon signed to Jeff and Mark Bass's FBT Productions, and recorded his debut album Infinite

Eminem cooked and washed dishes for minimum wage at Gilbert's Lodge, a family-style restaurant at St. Clair Shores

Detroit disc jockeys largely ignored Infinite, and the feedback Eminem did receive ("Why don't you go into rock and roll?") led him to craft angrier, moodier tracks

After the release of Infinite, his personal problems and substance abuse culminated in a suicide attempt

1997-1999 - Intro to slim shady

In the spring of 1997 he recorded his debut EP, the Slim Shady EP, which was released that winter by Web Entertainment.

After he was evicted from his home, Eminem went to Los Angeles to compete in the 1997 Rap Olympics, an annual, nationwide battle rap competition and won the second place in it.

Impressed by M&Ms mixtapes Dr.Dre decided to sign him to the label(Aftermath)

Slim shady lp turned out to be one of the most selling album of the year.

2000 - 2008 - Eminem's Early discography

After the slim shady lp emeinem released several platinum ( couple of diamand ) selling albums.(MMLP,The Eminem Show,Encore,Relapse)

Eminem also involved in lyrical beefs with Ja Rule and Benzino which allegedly resulted in the end of their musical careers

Desean aka proof ,Eminem's childhood friend was murdered in a shootout during this period.

His albums Encore and Relapse are rated below his initial albums by the critics inspite of their sales

Eminem had a near death expirience due to drug overdose which was followed by him going to rehab

Eminem received several law suites due to his lyrical content

2008 - now - The "new" Eminem

Recovery was supposedly eminems comeback album after bad reception of Relapse and Encore

Critics still haven't rated any of the albums after 2009 anywhere near MMLP or Eminem Show, with Revival receiving the worst rewiews and MMLP2 comparitively better than the others.

In 2018 Eminem expressed his outrage towards the hip hop industry and critics with the release of his album Kamikaze
